## GitHub Account Types
### Personal Account
Personal account is the regular accout for every user on **GitHub** and forms the identity of a user.
With a personal account, you have the ability to perform most of the tasks like creating repositories, packages, and projects. You have even the ability to mange permission on your resources. 
Other activities like, creating an issue on others repos and reviewing pull requests are assosicated with your personal account.

Personal accounts can either be GitHub Free or GitHub Pro, where Free account is limited feature set on thier private repos.
